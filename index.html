Playstation Vue's Search Function

Playstation Vue is a streaming service, comparable to Netflix and Hulu. Although it has a
nice selection of movies and TV shows, it still has a few irritating features in its interface.
One example is its search function. Instead of having a traditional keyboard, we are given the
alphabet and numbers 0-9, laid out vertically. Since this is a streaming service on a playstation
you have to select the letter/or number one by one by moving an on-screen selector. My aim
was to replace this function with a keyboard that is sorted in a way where the most common
characters were closer together. This would make the experience a smoother process for those
searching for specific movies and shows with their controller.
Research shows most users prefer alphabet keyboards over QWERTY keyboard when searching
on gaming consoles. To avoid situations like clicking the "over"button 19 times to search for a
movie that begins with 'S', my design places these letters at the beginning, and the rest is aligned alphabetically.
